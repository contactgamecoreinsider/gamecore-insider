Best Settings for Performance in 2026

Hardware is faster than ever, but software demands have kept pace. To achieve a stable 60 or 144 FPS, you need to master upscaling technologies like DLSS and FSR.

Upscaling vs. Native

Running a game at Native 4K is becoming a luxury. Quality-mode upscaling often provides a sharper image than native thanks to advanced AI reconstruction. We recommend 'Quality' mode for 1440p and 'Balanced' for 4K.

Anti-Aliasing

If you aren't using an upscaler, stick to TAA or SMAA. Avoid MSAA as it is extremely taxing on modern deferred renderers without providing a proportional visual boost.
