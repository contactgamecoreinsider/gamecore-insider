Marathon (2026) Starter Guide: Best Factions and Early Upgrades

Published: March 28, 2026 | By GameCore Staff

Bungie has returned to its roots with Marathon, a high-stakes extraction shooter that favors strategy over mindless shooting. For new players landing on Tau Ceti IV, the learning curve can be brutal. Here is how to survive your first five extractions.

Choosing Your Faction

In the early game, your faction determines your starting gear and passive buffs. We recommend starting with the MIDA Synthetic faction for their increased stamina regen—crucial for escaping geared-up squads.

Pro Tip: Don't hoard your gear! In Marathon, the economy moves fast. Use your best weapons early to secure high-tier loot drops rather than losing them to a random ambush later.

Essential Inventory Management

Space is limited. Always prioritize 'Core Fragments' over basic materials. One fragment can fund three full loadouts, making it the most efficient item for new players to extract with.
