The Future of Neural Rendering

We are entering the era of Neural Rendering. Instead of manually calculating every light ray, AI is now "predicting" what the next frame should look like.

Path Tracing

Unlike standard Ray Tracing, Path Tracing simulates the true behavior of light. It requires massive power, but through neural networks, even mid-range cards are beginning to handle these visuals in real-time through Frame Generation.
