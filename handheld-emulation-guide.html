Handheld Emulation: The New Frontier

Handheld PCs have revolutionized how we play. Emulation on the go is no longer limited to 16-bit classics; we are now seeing fluid 60 FPS performance for Wii U and Switch titles on mobile hardware.

Shader Compilation

Whether you are on a dedicated handheld or a high-end Android phone, shader compilation is your biggest enemy. Using pre-compiled shaders or 'Async' compilation modes can eliminate the "compilation stutter" that plagues early gameplay.
