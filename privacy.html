Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

At GameCore Insider, we prioritize the privacy of our visitors. This document outlines the types of information we collect and how we use it.

1. Log Files

Like most websites, we use log files. These files merely log visitors to the site—usually a standard procedure for hosting companies and a part of hosting services' analytics. The information inside includes IP addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, and referring/exit pages.

2. Cookies and Web Beacons

GameCore Insider uses 'cookies' to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. The information is used to optimize the users' experience by customizing our web page content.

3. Google DoubleClick DART Cookie

Google is one of the third-party vendors on our site. It also uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to our site and other sites on the internet.

4. Contact Us

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at contactgamecoreinsider@gmail.com.
