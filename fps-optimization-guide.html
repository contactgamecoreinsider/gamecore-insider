Ultimate FPS Optimization Guide

To get the most out of your rig, you need to go beyond the "Ultra" preset. Optimization is about balance—finding where to cut corners without ruining the visual experience.

Windows Level Tweaks

Start by enabling 'Game Mode' in Windows settings and ensuring your 'Power Plan' is set to High Performance. More importantly, use a clean installation tool like DDU for your drivers to fix stuttering issues.

In-Game Settings

Shadow quality and Volumetric Lighting are usually the biggest resource hogs. Dropping these from 'Ultra' to 'High' can often net a 20% performance boost with almost no visible difference.
