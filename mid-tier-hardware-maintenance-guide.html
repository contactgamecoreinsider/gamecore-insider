Keeping Your Mid-Tier Rig Cool: A 2026 Maintenance Guide

Published: March 28, 2026 | By GameCore Staff

If you're gaming on a mid-tier graphics card, thermal management is your best friend. Heat causes thermal throttling, which is the #1 reason for sudden FPS drops in modern games.

Essential 2026 Maintenance Steps

  • Dusting: Use compressed air once every 3 months.
  • Driver Cleanup: Use DDU (Display Driver Uninstaller) once a year for a fresh start.
  • Thermal Paste: If your card is over 2 years old, consider a repaste to drop temps by up to 10°C.

Software Checks

Always monitor your fan curves. Modern mid-range cards often favor "silent" modes that let the card get too hot. Switch to a "Performance" curve to ensure your hardware stays under 75°C during intense EA FC sessions.
